Newsletter Launch

I created a marketing plan to launch a new Newsletter for one of the Bone & Joint Publishing Journals


Marketing Objectives

  • Create a marketing plan for the launch of the new newsletter focused on health-tech
  • Create an onward plan for how to convert these newsletter subscribers to paid users.

My Stategy

I developed a two-step marketing strategy
  • IDENTIFY & ATTRACT the ideal reader for the newsletter
  • CONVERT by offering incentives to subscribe
Phase 1: IDENTIFY & ATTRACT

The main issues I tried to address were to find the best target audience for the newsletter both organically and via paid promotion and to attract and nurture your new audience after conversion. First I created a form where our existing audience was able to opt-in to receive the new newsletter. Then, I promoted the new newsetter to our existing list via email marketing, a series of Twitter posts and by adding a banner to our website with direct link to the form. I advertised the most popular post to lookalike audience and started collecting fresh email addresses of those interested in the subject of the newsletter.Add email addresses on Twitter and started doing ads to retarget those who didn't sign up. When eventually the list of sign ups grew to 1k it was time to start the next phase.

Phase 2: CONVERT

Start an automation email sequence targeting the new acquired newsletter sign-ups and offer a discount valid for a limited time. At the same time website launched a referral program to extend to all subscribers.

Automation email sequence & socials
  • EMAIL#1: Select the most popular articles plus free access articles for a limited time, related videos and podcasts. Plus, promoting the advantages of subscription.
  • EMAIL#2: Send a special offer only valid for 48h proposing a discount on an annual subscription only valid for those who signed up to the newsletter and any friend who they wanted to refer.
  • Social media: Promoting the discount on subscription to those who wanted to sign up to the health tech newletter.
